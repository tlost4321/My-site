Here are some projects that I have completed!

  • Boulder BrewHub

    Boulder BrewHub is a site to help you find a brewery in your town that carries the type of beer you are craving.

    For the search there are many different types of beer to choose from

    If you want to see all breweries around that have your type of beer on tap, just head to the Beer Types Page.

    At the top of the page you will find the different flavor profiles that many beers can have. If you choose a beer with that flavor it will push you down to the beer.

    Once you get to the beer you want. You will see it's flavor characteristics and which breweries in all Boulder surrounding areas that carry it.

    If you would like to see which breweries are in each area surrounding Boulder, head to the Breweries/Tap Rooms Page.

    Here you can scroll through all of the breweries that the Boulder area has to offer
